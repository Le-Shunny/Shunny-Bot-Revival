About Kurumi Bot

Kurumi Bot is a project that I have been working on lately. It's an AI-powered chatbot designed to assist users with various tasks and answer their questions. The name "Kurumi" means "walnut" in Japanese, symbolizing the idea of a bot with a smart and compact core.

Kurumi Bot is programmed with advanced natural language processing and machine learning techniques, allowing it to understand user input and provide relevant and helpful responses. It can handle a wide range of queries, from general knowledge questions to task automation.

My inspiration for creating Kurumi Bot came from my fascination with AI and its potential to simplify our lives. I wanted to develop a bot that could not only showcase the power of AI but also be genuinely useful to people in their day-to-day activities.

I am continuously working on improving Kurumi Bot, adding new features, and enhancing its capabilities. My goal is to make it a reliable and user-friendly virtual assistant that can be accessed easily across various platforms.
